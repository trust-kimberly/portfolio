A Long Shot

alongshot.xyz

2020

In the final semester of my graduate degree, the onset of the pandemic was marked by the sudden loss of access to glassmaking studios needed to complete my thesis body of work. I pivoted in the midst of the first shutdown by learning to code in order to give myself a material I could work safely with from quarantine.
