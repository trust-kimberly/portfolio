Skin Studies

Blown glass, snap pea, soil, grow light

2018

I began to think about tactility through a sentient life’s skin, and found that a growing snap pea that came into contact with a sharp edge soon after contact would remove itself.

The sprout would continue to grow away from the sharpness until its weight became too much to hold without a support structure, at which point it ultimately returned to rest and brush against the razor edge.
