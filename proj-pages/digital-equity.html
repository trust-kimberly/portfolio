Digital Equity Plan

mapc.github.io/digital-equity-report

2022

Designed and developed a digital toolkit alongside Metropolitan Area Planning Council's civic technologists and data analysts to share resources and inform decisions around municipal broadband access. The plan includes over 30 interactive data visualizations created using Tableau and Mapbox. The site itself was built and deployed using React and Gatsby.
